Box Model:
		Border
			Solid
			Dotted
			Ridge
			Double
		Border-radius
		
		Margin -a margin is the space around an element's border,
			Marign Left / Right / Top / Bottom
		Padding -padding is the space between an element's border and the element's content
			Padding Left / Right / Top / Bottom
In simple way:
the margin property controls the space outside an element, 
and the padding property controls the space inside an element.
